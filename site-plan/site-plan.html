Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to form an online "cheatsheet" of the toki pona language and associated writing system, complete with external links, dictionary, and input box. (The input box does nothing, except to display what you type in the linja pona 4.9 font.) Specifically: - The purpose of the main page is to catch the eye of the reader, providing links to the other pages. It has minimal content compared to the other two pages, and is meant to look pretty. - The purpose of the first subpage is to link many different learning resources, providing a breif description for each. - The purpose of the second subpage is to talk more in-depth about symbologies in the language, providing an input box that renders text into sitelen pona, the graphical writing system that toki pona uses.

Audience

The audience of thise website includes those who are new to toki pona, who want a comprehensive cheatsheet, as well as those have intermediate experience who are looking to broaden their knowlege with further media.
