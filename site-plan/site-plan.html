Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to form an online "cheatsheet" of the toki pona language and associated writing system, complete with external links, dictionary, and input box. (The input box does nothing, except to display what you type in the linja pona 4.9 font.)

Audience

The audience of thise website includes those who are new to toki pona, who want a comprehensive cheatsheet, as well as those have intermediate experience who are looking to broaden their knowlege with further media.
